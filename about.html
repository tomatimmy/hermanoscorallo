Hermanos Corallo,

A life in two lifes

A humanist photographer and a lover of sound leave Buenos Aires with two cameras, to tour Latin America in search of stories to portray. They retrace the path together, crossing lives and characters whom they unfreeze in photo and video, revealing the world around them and their stories in various humanitarian projects.
Thus, the world returns double and asks for quadruple, making each step part of the change, inside and outside of them. ​ “We understood that the only way to be free is to help someone else to be… 7 years later we continue with the same dream, but now together with a great team of professionals ready to accompany us on each adventure, helping us to continue telling stories” To learn more about them:
